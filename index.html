About Our System

This platform ensures fair distribution of government schemes to farmers using AI-based impact scoring, district quota allocation, and reservation support. It removes corruption, manual bias, and ensures the most deserving farmers receive benefits first.

How the System Works

1. Farmer Applies

Enter land size, income and district.

2. Eligibility Check

System checks scheme eligibility automatically.

3. AI Ranking

Impact score prioritizes vulnerable farmers.

4. Allocation

Scheme distributed based on merit and quota.
